Planning to cross from Lombok to Bali by **fastboat**? You've probably been Googling around and still feel confused — which operator is best, what time do they depart, how much does it cost, and what do you need to prepare?

This article covers everything you need to know: route options, pricing, and tips to make your first fastboat trip smooth and drama-free.

## Why pick fastboat over flying or ferry?

There are 3 common ways to cross Lombok–Bali:

| Mode | Duration | Price (PP) | Comfort |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Fastboat** | 1.5–2.5 hours | Rp 300K–900K | 😀 Comfortable |
| Standard Ferry (Lembar–Padang Bai) | 4–5 hours | Rp 50K–80K | 😐 Slow |
| Flight (LOP–DPS) | 30 min + transit | Rp 800K–2M | 😀 Fast but expensive |

**Fastboat wins on the price-time balance.** Perfect for backpackers, honeymooners, and family groups alike.

## Most popular routes

From Lombok, there are 5 main departure ports:

1. **Gili Trawangan** → Padang Bai / Sanur / Serangan
2. **Gili Air** → Padang Bai / Nusa Penida
3. **Gili Meno** → Padang Bai (rare)
4. **Bangsal (Bangsal Harbour)** → Padang Bai / Serangan
5. **Senggigi** → Padang Bai

Most frequented route: **Gili Trawangan → Padang Bai** (±1h 15m duration, from Rp 300K).

## How much is a fastboat ticket?

Typical price range:

- **Rp 300K–450K** → standard operators (economical, basic facilities)
- **Rp 500K–700K** → mid-tier (AC, snacks, life jacket)
- **Rp 750K–900K** → premium (WiFi, meals, more comfortable seating)

**Saving tip**: booking online is usually Rp 50K–100K cheaper than buying directly at the port counter.

## Departure schedules

Most operators depart in the **morning to early afternoon (07:00–14:00)**. After 3 PM, departures stop because of higher waves.

Most common departure windows:
- **08:00–10:00** — peak time, boats often full
- **11:00–12:00** — relatively empty, good if you're checking out late
- **13:00–14:00** — last call, fewer operators available

**Pro tip**: book a morning slot (08:00–09:00) so you have more daylight to explore Bali on arrival.

## What is Harbour Tax?

**Harbour Tax** is a port retribution fee of **Rp 20,000 per passenger** paid at the departure port.

Important:
- ✅ **Not included** in the ticket price
- ✅ Paid **in cash at the port** before boarding
- ✅ Applies at **Padang Bai, Serangan, Gili Trawangan, Bangsal, Senggigi**
- ✅ On tapToGo, you can choose to pay via the app or at the port (default off)

Make sure to have Rp 20K × number of passengers in cash before departure.

## Check-in tips for smooth boarding

1. **Arrive at least 30 minutes before departure** — for boarding pass + luggage line
2. **Bring original ID** (KTP/Passport) matching the booking
3. **Max luggage 20kg per person** — excess weight has extra fees
4. **Wear comfortable clothes & sandals**, not high heels (boarding via wobbly gangways)
5. **Take anti-nausea pills** (Dramamine, Antimo) — fastboats rock heavily if windy
6. **Charge your phone fully** — some boats have outlets, but not all
7. **Bring a jacket** — boat AC can be aggressively cold

## What happens if weather is bad?

Operators reserve the right to **cancel or delay** if:
- Wave height > 2.5m
- Strong winds
- Port authority closes operations for safety

If the operator cancels:
- **100% refund** or free reschedule
- WhatsApp notification 2–4 hours before

## For Children & Babies

- **Babies < 2 years**: usually free (on parent's lap), baby life jacket required (provided by operator)
- **Children 2–12 years**: full ticket (most operators don't offer child discount)
- Small strollers OK in cargo, large strollers need pre-confirmation

## Frequently asked questions

**Is fastboat travel safe?**
All official operators are required to have full life jackets, navigation licenses, and passenger insurance. As long as you book with a legitimate operator (not an informal scalper), it's safe.

**Can I book at the last minute (H-1)?**
Yes, but no guarantee your preferred time slot will still be available. **Recommendation: book 2–3 days ahead** for the best time selection.

**Are there night fastboats?**
No. All operators run daytime only (morning to afternoon) for safety reasons.

**How long until booking confirmation?**
Maximum **15 minutes** during operating hours (07:00–22:00 WITA). Outside those hours, confirmation by next morning.
